Dome & Hexagon Pyramid

Glass Dome

Boards (2×4 = 3.5"×1.5")

A hexagonal pyramid (6 base boards + 6 rafters to an apex) sitting on the floor. Two clearances matter: the base (lip) and the sloped roof (dome shoulder).

How It Sits

The dome sits on the yurt roof, so the wood frame can sit lower. 0 = flush (frame base level with the dome rim); higher = frame sits lower / dome rides higher above it, which tightens the roof clearance.
